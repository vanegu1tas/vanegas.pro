Hi! :)

My name is Daniel Vanegas, I’m a graphic designer from Colombia. I spent the last few years working in different kinds of projects, developing my skills as a UI designer focused in tech industry. All my life, I’ve felt empathy with technology, but I didn’t know how to create it. I made my research and I found this UX/UI world and I realized how I can create technology from the design perspective.

Working as a freelance designer focused on digital products, I had the chance to work along with a developer all over this years, and that helped me to understand how to communicate my design ideas to a tech person and work together as a team. Having worked with a developer motivated me to learn html, css and Git on my own.

Made with ♥ in Bogotá, Colombia.
