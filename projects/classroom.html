Classroom - Crehana

It's not just a player; it's our study environment.

Role: UI Designer Year: 2023

Contact me

Project context

Our classroom is one of the most important sections on the platform. According to the data collected, it's where our students spend the most time. This has allowed us to gather a lot of information and feedback to provide them with an experience that is based on their studying needs.



Classroom structure

The classroom structure has two important sections, our player and our sidebar. Each one of this sections got differents functions that help our students in a smooth way to increase their knowledge.



Player section

This section not only plays videos but also provides proper support for the various types of classes we offer at Crehana, such as SCORM, PDF, URL-based classes, and assessments.









Sidebar

The sidebar helps students track their progress in the course, as well as allowing them to take notes, leave comments within the community, download course materials, and report issues.









Assesments in the classroom

When the classes are assessment-based, our player adapts and renders the assessment questions depending on the type configured by the administrator. Currently, we have text-based questions, image-based questions, markdown questions.









Surveys and NPS

The administrator can set up a simple survey for students to complete at the end of the course to measure the students satisfaction and capture their responses and comments for further analysis.
